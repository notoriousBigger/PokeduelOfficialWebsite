About Pokémon and Their Attacks

  • Types of Attacks:
    • Physical Attacks: These moves rely on the Attack stat of the Pokémon and include moves like Tackle, Bite, and Earthquake.
    • Special Attacks: These moves use the Special Attack stat and include moves like Thunderbolt, Flamethrower, and Surf.
    • Status Moves: These moves do not deal direct damage but can cause status effects or alter stats. Examples include Toxic, Sleep Powder, and Swords Dance.
  • Attack Types:
    • Normal: Common moves with no particular strengths or weaknesses (e.g., Tackle, Scratch).
    • Fire: Strong against Grass, Bug, Ice, and Steel types, but weak against Water, Rock, and Fire types (e.g., Flamethrower, Fire Blast).
    • Water: Effective against Fire, Ground, and Rock types, but less effective against Water, Grass, and Dragon types (e.g., Water Gun, Hydro Pump).
    • Grass: Powerful against Water, Ground, and Rock types, but weak against Fire, Grass, Poison, Flying, Bug, and Dragon types (e.g., Vine Whip, Solar Beam).
    • Electric: Strong against Water and Flying types, but weak against Electric, Grass, Ground, and Dragon types (e.g., Thunder Shock, Thunderbolt).
    • Ice: Effective against Grass, Ground, Flying, and Dragon types, but weak against Fire, Water, Ice, and Steel types (e.g., Ice Beam, Blizzard).
    • Fighting: Strong against Normal, Ice, Rock, Dark, and Steel types, but weak against Poison, Flying, Psychic, Bug, Ghost, and Fairy types (e.g., Karate Chop, Close Combat).
    • Poison: Effective against Grass and Fairy types, but weak against Poison, Ground, Rock, and Ghost types (e.g., Poison Sting, Sludge Bomb).
    • Ground: Strong against Fire, Electric, Poison, Rock, and Steel types, but weak against Grass, Bug, and Flying types (e.g., Earthquake, Dig).
    • Flying: Effective against Grass, Fighting, and Bug types, but weak against Electric, Rock, and Steel types (e.g., Fly, Aerial Ace).
    • Psychic: Strong against Fighting and Poison types, but weak against Psychic, Steel, and Dark types (e.g., Confusion, Psychic).
    • Bug: Effective against Grass, Psychic, and Dark types, but weak against Fire, Fighting, Poison, Flying, Ghost, and Steel types (e.g., Bug Bite, X-Scissor).
    • Rock: Strong against Fire, Ice, Flying, and Bug types, but weak against Fighting, Ground, and Steel types (e.g., Rock Throw, Stone Edge).
    • Ghost: Effective against Psychic and Ghost types, but weak against Dark types (e.g., Shadow Ball, Night Shade).
    • Dragon: Strong against Dragon types, but weak against Steel types and Fairy types (e.g., Dragon Claw, Draco Meteor).
    • Dark: Effective against Psychic and Ghost types, but weak against Fighting, Dark, and Fairy types (e.g., Bite, Dark Pulse).
    • Steel: Strong against Ice, Rock, and Fairy types, but weak against Fire, Water, Electric, and Steel types (e.g., Iron Tail, Flash Cannon).
    • Fairy: Effective against Fighting, Dragon, and Dark types, but weak against Poison and Steel types (e.g., Moonblast, Dazzling Gleam).
  • Move Effects:
    • Damage-Dealing Moves: These moves directly reduce the opponent's HP (e.g., Thunderbolt, Earthquake).
    • Status Moves: These moves can inflict status conditions like paralysis, sleep, poison, or burn (e.g., Thunder Wave, Toxic).
    • Stat-Altering Moves: These moves can raise or lower the stats of the user or the opponent (e.g., Swords Dance, Growl).
    • Healing Moves: These moves restore the user's HP (e.g., Recover, Synthesis).
