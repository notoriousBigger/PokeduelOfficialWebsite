About Pokémon

Pokémon, short for "Pocket Monsters," are fictional creatures that humans, known as Pokémon Trainers, catch and train to battle each other for sport. Here are some key points about Pokémon:

1) Origins: Pokémon was created by Satoshi Tajiri and Ken Sugimori and was first released as a pair of video games for the original Game Boy in 1996.

2) Types: Each Pokémon belongs to one or two of 18 different types, such as Fire, Water, Grass, and Electric. These types determine the strengths and weaknesses of each Pokémon in battle.

3) Evolution: Many Pokémon can evolve into a stronger form, gaining new abilities and increased stats.

4) Moves: Pokémon can learn a variety of moves, each associated with a particular type. Moves have different effects in battle, from causing damage to inflicting status conditions.

5) Stats: Each Pokémon has six key stats that affect its performance in battle: HP (Health Points), Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, and Speed.

In Poke Duel, you'll get to experience the thrill of battling with these classic creatures from the original Generation 1 lineup. Whether you're a seasoned Pokémon Trainer or new to the world of Pokémon, there's always something exciting to discover!
